The Fun They Had – Summary

"The Fun They Had" is written by Isaac Asimov. The story shows future education where children study with mechanical teachers. Margie and Tommy read about old schools where children studied together with human teachers.

Margie dislikes her mechanical teacher because it gives her too much homework. After reading about old schools, she feels that children in the past had more fun while learning.
